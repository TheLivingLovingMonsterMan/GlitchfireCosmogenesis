PREFACE
────────────────────────────────────────
Where the Mirrorwell reveals the past, the **Starlight Loom** threads futures-in‑waiting. 
This atlas provides librarians and players with 24 constellation overlays ready for use 
with the Zyphor Lens or any metaphoric night sky. Each pattern can be woven into the air 
with the Loom to project map‑overlays that highlight hidden Vorshents, sealed doors, or 
subterranean echo strands.

I. FUNDAMENTALS OF STARLIGHT WEAVING
────────────────────────────────────────
Primary Glyph Anchor: 🖭 (Loom Quill)  
Auxiliary Glyphs: ⚡ (Zyphor Pulse) to energize threads, ● (Orbix) to mark nodes.  
Rule of Twelve: Every overlay must fit within a 12×12 celestial grid to maintain 
resonance with the library’s master star‑map.

Casting Sequence:
  1. Hold the Loom Quill aloft; trace ⚡ to prime.
  2. Speak the anchor phrase: “Threads awake, align the void.”
  3. Draw nodes (●) in order given by the pattern script.
  4. Finalize with ∴ to seal the overlay.

────────────────────────────────────────
II. OVERLAY GRID SYSTEM
────────────────────────────────────────
The atlas uses a simple coordinate grid:
  • X‑axis: A → L (left to right)  
  • Y‑axis: 1 → 12 (bottom to top)

Example cell reference: **E7** (column E, row 7). Overlay ASCII diagrams place ★ at 
node positions, with optional ─│╲╱ glyphs suggesting ideal thread paths. Librarians 
may redraw paths freely; node order is what matters.

IV. WEAVING TECHNIQUES & PATTERN SCRIPTS
────────────────────────────────────────
• **Single‑Pass Weave**: one continuous motion; best for Codes ★1, ⚡4  
• **Cross‑Stitch Weave**: intersecting diagonals; use for Ourobouros, Mantis  
• **Echo‑Latch Weave**: pause after each node; mandatory for 𓂀‑tag overlays  
Failure to use the indicated technique increases risk of Thread Snag (see Event Table).

────────────────────────────────────────
V. INTEGRATION NOTES
────────────────────────────────────────
• **Zyphor Lens**: place lens over the active overlay; the lens light amplifies ★ nodes, 
  projecting map rooms as glowing doorways.
• **Mirrorwell Interaction**: Echo Lynx or any 𓂀 overlay auto‑logs a Reflection Token 
  tied to current location depth.

────────────────────────────────────────
VI. RANDOM STARFALL EVENT TABLE
────────────────────────────────────────
Roll 1d6 after completing an overlay outdoors:
  1  Star‑Bleed — overlay distorts, spawning 1 Flenqua (CR 1)
  2  Shooting Echo — gain 1 free Reflection Token
  3  Prism Twist — colors invert; next Insight check at disadvantage
  4  Silent Shower — triggers Law XIII silence for 3 minutes
  5  Sky‑Cipher — hidden message appears in overlay
  6  Aurora Burst — party gains +1 Inspiration

VII. SAMPLE HUNT — “Whispering Nebula”
────────────────────────────────────────
Quest  • Find the Vorshent named **Echo‑Whisp** hiding in a collapsed observatory.  
Step 1 • Weave The Whisper Hook inside the ruined dome (Difficulty 12).  
Step 2 • Decode sky‑cipher cliffs revealed by Zyphor Lens.  
Step 3 • Use Echo Lynx to pinpoint Mirrorwell depth of Echo‑Whisp fragment (expect depth L‑2).  
Rewards • Constellation gem (+1 glyph slot) and Nyx Aurora favor.
