• **Tri‑Spiral Lattice**: three intertwined overlays; requires three players weaving
  in synchronized motion.
• **Pulse‑Echo Resonance**: blend a ⚡ overlay with a 𓂀 overlay to create a stable
  star‑portal lasting 3 turns.

────────────────────────────────────────
II. HARMONIC OVERLAY MESH
────────────────────────────────────────
Combining two or more overlays with compatible node matrices produces a **Mesh Score**.
Sum absolute grid offsets; a score ≤12 is stable, 13‑16 risky, >16 catastrophic.

IV. REACTIVE WEAVING & GLYPH RESONANCE
────────────────────────────────────────
Mesh overlays inherit effects of all component patterns plus a resonance bonus:
  • Same Glyph Families (e.g., two ⚡ patterns) grant +2 Mesh Stability.
  • Opposing Glyphs (⚡ and 𝕊) risk chain‑reaction on roll ≤4 (d20).

────────────────────────────────────────
V. CROSS‑REALM NEXUS PROTOCOLS
────────────────────────────────────────
1. Align realm’s local star grid via Zyphor Lens calibration sequence.
2. Weave Tri‑Spiral Lattice at anchor point.
3. Activate Pulse‑Echo Resonance while chanting *“Lights unbound, paths entwined”*.
4. Step through the nexus portal before Mesh Score timer expires (3 turns).

VI. CELESTIAL ANOMALY TABLE
────────────────────────────────────────

────────────────────────────────────────

Orbix Wound vault. Requires stable Mesh Score ≤10 across three realms.
