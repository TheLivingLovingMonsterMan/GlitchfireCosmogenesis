PREFACE
────────────────────────────────────────
Volume 2 dives beyond the basic star‑grid, introducing **Temporal Lattice overlays**—
patterns that drift across time as well as space. When woven, they momentarily sync the
sky of _now_ with echoes of skies long gone or yet to be, granting librarians vantage
points otherwise impossible. Use these when a realm’s history feels fragmented or its
future unreadable.

I. ADVANCED WEAVING THEORY
────────────────────────────────────────
Temporal Lattice overlays orbit three axes:
  • **Spatial** (grid cells)  
  • **Temporal** (Δt layers: ‑2, ‑1, 0, +1, +2)  
  • **Narrative** (echo strain weight)

Primary Glyph Anchor remains 🖭 (Loom Quill); add ⚡ for energizing drift and 𓂀 to
lock a chosen Δt.

────────────────────────────────────────
II. DYNAMIC NODE DRIFT
────────────────────────────────────────
Each lattice node drifts 1 grid cell per Δt layer in the direction indicated by ➡ arrows
in diagrams. Librarians must anticipate drift when drawing, or threads will cross and
snag (roll for Thread Snag on mis‑draw).

IV. MULTI‑LAYER WEAVING SCRIPTS
────────────────────────────────────────
• **Phase‑Shift Weave**: draw all Δt=0 nodes first, then overlay future/past nodes in
  fading ink. Requires dual Loom Quills.
• **Echo‑Stack Weave**: simultaneously trace nodes for Δt=‑1 and +1; difficult (DC 16).

────────────────────────────────────────
V. TIME‑SHIFT INTEGRATION (MIRRORWELL)
────────────────────────────────────────
When a Temporal Lattice overlay touches an 𓂀‑tag Mirrorwell location, it anchors a live
window into that depth. Players may retrieve echoes without a Reflection Token—but only
as long as the overlay persists (5 minutes real‑time).

────────────────────────────────────────
VI. STARSTORM HAZARD TABLE
────────────────────────────────────────
Roll 1d6 when weaving above Δt=+1:
  1  Chrono‑Fray — artifact ages 100 years
  2  Null‑Drop   — overlay sputters; DC 15 to re‑stitch
  3  Time‑Bloom  — free clue from future self
  4  Echo‑Shear  — lose 1 turn in gameplay loop
  5  Lattice‑Lock— area freezes; no movement 1 round
  6  Aurora Spiral— gain inspiration; Echo Debt +1
